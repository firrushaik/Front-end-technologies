The Solar System

    Our solar system is made up of the Sun and eight planets that orbit,
    as well as a numereous moons,asteroids,and comets.The Sun is a star
    that contains over 99% of the system's mass.The four inner planets
    are rocky, while the four outer planets are gas giants.
